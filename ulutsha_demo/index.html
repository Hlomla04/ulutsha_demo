About Us

Rooted in Brotherhood,
Built on Trust

Ulutsha LwaseMancotsheni Burial Society was founded in 2026 by and for young men of eMancotsheni. Every active member contributes R100 per month. When a fellow member loses a family member, the whole society contributes R5,000 towards funeral expenses.

Membership is open to young men of eMancotsheni and surrounding areas. Executive members are elected by active members of the burial society through our WhatsApp group.

Our mission is to ease the burden of funeral costs through collective monthly contributions, ensuring no member faces hardship alone in their time of greatest need.

What We Offer

Monthly Contributions
Every active member contributes R100 per month. Payments are tracked digitally and members can view their full contribution history at any time through the portal.
Funeral Assistance
When an active member loses a family member, fellow members collectively contribute R5,000 in funeral support. Claims are reviewed and processed promptly by the executive committee.
Community Support
Membership connects you to a brotherhood of young men. Executives are elected by active members on our WhatsApp group, keeping leadership transparent and community-driven.
